Sci-fi list of recommendations

If you enjoy sci-fi, I strongly recommend these works of sci-fi art. I recommend them even if you don't think you like sci-fi.

Image credit

2001: A Space Odyssey

1968

A contemplative exploration of near future space exploration, the perils of AI and the possiblities of non-human intelligent life.

Homeworld series

1999-2024

A series of strategy games follow the last remnant of a people searching for where they came from. They rediscover clues to their past that showed they came from another world, and in the process of exploring that past their world is destroyed as they set a course among the stars towards that place their ancestors came from. Samuel Barber's 1936 Adagio for Strings is the definining musical theme.

Bloodchild - by Octavia Butler

1995

This is a short story examining the relationship between a human and an alien, and coming to grips with the reality of that relationship.

The Abyss

1989

A film by James Cameron following a group of researchers working on the ocean floor, the discovery they make when they assist with the search for a missing military submarine and navigating the response

Alien film series

1979-2024

A series of films highlighting the exploitation of workers by corporations by the vain attempts to contain and control an alien life form that utilizes humans and other life to reproduce. This exemplifies blue collar dirty space in direct contrast with Star Trek and other clean space stories.
