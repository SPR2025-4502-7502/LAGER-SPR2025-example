Places I've paddled

From paddling on Lake Winnebago to Seagull Lake to Lake Superior and points between I've traversed a wide variety of waters by canoe.

Of a chilly June trip I swamped my canoe, and we had to swim to the shore. This changed our trip from a week long loop to a series of day trips. Other trips did not start off so dramatically.
